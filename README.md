<h2>MediConnect – Healthcare Appointment & Telemedicine Platform</h2><br>
<h3>📌 Project Overview</h3>

MediConnect is a healthcare management platform designed to simplify the process of booking doctor appointments, managing patient records, and providing telemedicine consultations.

This project was created as a Business Analysis case study to demonstrate the complete Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), from business requirement gathering to software documentation.

<h3>🎯 Business Problem</h3>

Patients often experience:

Long waiting times for appointments
Difficulty finding suitable doctors
Lack of centralized medical records
Limited access to online consultations
Manual appointment management

MediConnect aims to digitize these processes through an integrated healthcare platform.

<h3>🎯 Project Objectives</h3>
Simplify appointment booking
Enable secure patient registration
Support online video consultations
Manage electronic medical records
Improve communication between doctors and patients
Reduce administrative workload
